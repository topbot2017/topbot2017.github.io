ABOUT THE EVENT

TOPBOT Aparoksha 2017

IIIT Allahabad, a powerhouse Institute in the field of IT has grown leaps and bounds over the past few years and is placed among the elite colleges in the country. Aparoksha, the annual technical festival of IIIT-A promises to be a fun-filled, eventfull extravaganza. To be organized from 23rd to 25th March 2017, it has an array of events to test the technical skills of students coming in to participate from all around the country. IIIT Allahabad and Aparoksha presents to you TOPBOT, a two round robotics contest. This year, two interesting themes await you.
Bolt : an obstacle evading contest will require your bot to overcome all the hindrances put in front of it in order to stand above the rest.
Jarvis : is a restricted contest only for the first and second year students based on line follower theme.

So come in, show your engineering prowess and grab hold of the various prizes up for grabs.
